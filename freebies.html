Freebies

In order to support the poor and downtrodden youth, tuition fee is waived for all the trainees in Government ITIs. The following additional benefits are also provided as a motivation to the trainees to the Government ITI trainees


1.Bus pass from the place of residence to the institute
2.Two sets of uniform per year
3. One pair of shoe per year
4 Bi-Cycle
5. Monthly stipend @ Rs.750/- to all the trainees irrespective of community and income
6. Laptop to all the trainees
7. Text Books and drawing instruments
8. Identity Card
9. Raw materials for training purpose

Testing : All India Trade Test is conducted by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT) under Annual exam pattern.
Certification: Successful trainees are awarded with National Trade Certificate (NTC).
Employment: Self Employment or Job opportunities in industries as Technicians
