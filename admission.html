Admission

CTS Admission Process

Online counselling is conducted Statewide Selection is made on merit basis duly following rules of reservation. The candidates exercise the option of choosing the ITI and trade of their choice.

Students between the age of 14 - 40 are admitted in Industrial Training Institutes. No upper age limit for girls. Admission is made during the month of August every year.

Admission is purely based on merit i.e., marks scored in the School Examination, and candidates are admitted within the rule of reservation as follows:

Selection for admission : Based on merit

Engineering / Non Engineering Trades : Average Marks obtained in all subjects with Communal Reservation

Open competition : 31%

Backward Classes : 30%

Most Backward classes : 20%

Scheduled Castes : 18%

Scheduled Tribes : 1%

Physically Handicapped (within the BC/ MBC/SC/ST quota) Women (within the reserved quota) : 3%

Students from Rural areas : 15%

Parentless students : 2%

State level sports person : 1%
